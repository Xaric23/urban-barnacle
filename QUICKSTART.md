## ðŸ“Š Game Flow

1. **Recruit** performers (Singer, Dancer, DJ, Bartender, Security)
2. **Train** them to increase skill (costs energy)
3. **Promote** skilled performers for permanent team bonuses
4. **Upgrade** your club for better income and features
5. **Run nights** to earn money and reputation
6. **Manage** events, relationships, and finances
7. **Advance** days to trigger weekly economy changes
